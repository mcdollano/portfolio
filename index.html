About Me

My name is Mark. I'm from Naga City, Philippines. Creating my own stuff has always been my thing. I was extremely fortunate to land a job as a Marketing Officer. There, I was able to develop my management skills and most importantly, I was engaged and creative. Aside from scribbling down ideas, I enjoy solving problems. I value impact over anything else. Whenever I make a project, I ask myself if whether I can improve or solve an existing problem out of the things that I create.

I've always believed that thru computer programming, one can become a difference maker. That's why after 2.5 years, I decided to quit my corporate job to pursue what I consider a long-time "creative itch". I enrolled in Tuitt Coding Bootcamp Philippines, wherein students would undergo intensive training to become a full-stack web developer within 3 months. From a former marketing guy who haven't written a single line of computer code before, here I am, making my own stuff and doing something I am truly passionate about.
